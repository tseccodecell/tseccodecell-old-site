Weekly Challenges

Weekly Challenges are an initiative to encourage regular practice. Each week we select three problems of varying difficulty from different competetive programming websites.


We encourage you to find, implement and test various solutions to these. Feel free to Google concepts you don't know and discuss with your friends! At the end of the week, please fill the feedback form provided so we know what we can do better!
